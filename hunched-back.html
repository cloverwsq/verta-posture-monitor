üîç What's Happening?

When you hunch forward, your head moves ahead of your spine, creating extra pressure on your neck muscles. Over time, this can lead to:

  • Neck pain - Strained muscles from supporting your head's weight
  • Upper back tension - Rounded shoulders pulling on muscles
  • Headaches - Poor blood flow and muscle tension
  • Reduced lung capacity - Compressed chest limiting breathing

‚úÖ Quick Fixes

  • Roll your shoulders back - Imagine tucking your shoulder blades into your back pockets
  • Align your ears with shoulders - Your head should be directly above your spine, not jutting forward
  • Check your screen height - The top of your monitor should be at or slightly below eye level
  • Take micro-breaks - Stand up and stretch every 30 minutes
  • Strengthen your core - A strong core supports proper posture naturally
